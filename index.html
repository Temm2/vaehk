Join our more than 34,000+ Patient Cases. Be part of one of our clinical trials.

Data sharing may help doctors, researchers and institutions to identify the best cancer treatment and to find new methods for a cure.

Management Tool.

Track your treatment. Manage and give access to your palliative care team. Control medications, symptoms and activity. Get access to online aid, independent of your location. Give access to external physicians.

Predictive Model.

Based on our more than 34,000 patient cases, Vähk make suggestions on cases similar to yours. The aims is to produce research grade data which is useful also for researchers.

E-Health Record.

Show your doctor, detailed reports from your medication, tracked symptoms and activity. Explore together the options which may be most efficient nationally or internationally.

Evaluate with your oncologist the benefits of monitoring your treatment and medical history with Vähk today.
